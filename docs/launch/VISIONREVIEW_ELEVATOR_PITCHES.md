# VisionReview AI — Elevator Pitches

## 10-Second Pitch

VisionReview AI helps video teams move from raw footage to reviewed selects, QC notes, and client-ready handoff packages faster.

## 30-Second Pitch

VisionReview AI is an AI-assisted editorial review and client handoff workflow. It analyzes footage into segments, supports human review decisions, captures notes and tags, helps plan reel drafts, runs export QC, and generates client-ready review packages.

## 60-Second Pitch

VisionReview AI is a local beta full-stack application that combines AI-assisted video analysis with human editorial review. Instead of only generating clips, it helps teams review footage, mark clips as Favorite, Keep, or Reject, add notes and tags, compare reel candidates, run quality checks, and prepare client handoff packages.

## Best One-Liner

AI-assisted review-to-handoff for video teams.
