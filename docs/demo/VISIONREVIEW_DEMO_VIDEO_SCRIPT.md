# VisionReview AI — Demo Video Script

## Demo Goal

Create a clear 60–90 second walkthrough that positions VisionReview AI as an AI-assisted editorial review and client handoff workflow.

The demo should show that VisionReview AI is more than a clipping tool. It helps move from raw footage to reviewed selects, QC notes, client metadata, and client-ready deliverables.

---

## Recommended Demo Length

```text
60–90 seconds
```

A shorter demo is better for LinkedIn and portfolio viewers.

---

## Core Message

```text
VisionReview AI helps video teams move from raw footage to reviewed selects, draft structure, QC notes, and client-ready handoff packages faster.
```

---

## Demo Structure

### 1. Opening Hook — 5 seconds

Suggested voiceover:

```text
Most AI video tools focus on generating clips. VisionReview AI focuses on the full review-to-handoff workflow.
```

On screen:

- Show the VisionReview AI report page
- Briefly show video player and segment list

---

### 2. Segment Review — 10–15 seconds

Suggested voiceover:

```text
The app analyzes footage into reviewable segments with timestamps, transcripts, scores, and visual quality signals.
```

On screen:

- Scroll through segment cards
- Show scores
- Show transcript excerpt
- Show highlight candidate badge

---

### 3. Human Review Decisions — 10 seconds

Suggested voiceover:

```text
Editors can mark segments as Favorite, Keep, Reject, or Unreviewed, then add notes and tags to explain why a clip matters.
```

On screen:

- Open Review Mode
- Show a segment being selected
- Apply a tag such as Hook or B-roll
- Show tag pills on the segment card

---

### 4. Reel Builder — 10–15 seconds

Suggested voiceover:

```text
Reviewed segments flow into a Reel Builder where clips can be organized, compared, and prepared for a draft structure.
```

On screen:

- Show Reel Builder
- Show Order or Timeline tab
- Show Compare section
- Avoid lingering too long on dense UI

---

### 5. Export Quality Control — 10 seconds

Suggested voiceover:

```text
Before handoff, the Export Quality Control view flags issues like clip count, runtime, opening hook, and ending or CTA readiness.
```

On screen:

- Show QC tab
- Show export readiness card
- Show check cards

---

### 6. Client Metadata + Handoff — 15–20 seconds

Suggested voiceover:

```text
The Client tab captures project metadata, delivery branding, and client notes, then generates a client review HTML report and ZIP handoff package.
```

On screen:

- Show Client/Project Metadata
- Show Save Metadata and Last saved timestamp
- Show Client Review HTML button
- Show Download Client Package ZIP button
- Show Send-Ready Summary

---

### 7. Closing — 5 seconds

Suggested voiceover:

```text
VisionReview AI is a local beta workflow for AI-assisted editorial review, quality control, and client-ready video handoff.
```

On screen:

- Show final Client tab or public showcase README
- End with product name

---

## Full Voiceover Script

```text
Most AI video tools focus on generating clips. VisionReview AI focuses on the full review-to-handoff workflow.

The app analyzes footage into reviewable segments with timestamps, transcripts, scores, and visual quality signals.

Editors can mark segments as Favorite, Keep, Reject, or Unreviewed, then add notes and tags to explain why a clip matters.

Reviewed segments flow into a Reel Builder where clips can be organized, compared, and prepared for a draft structure.

Before handoff, the Export Quality Control view flags issues like clip count, runtime, opening hook, and ending or CTA readiness.

The Client tab captures project metadata, delivery branding, and client notes, then generates a client review HTML report and ZIP handoff package.

VisionReview AI is a local beta workflow for AI-assisted editorial review, quality control, and client-ready video handoff.
```

```text
VisionReview AI is an AI-assisted video review and client handoff workflow.

It analyzes footage into reviewable segments, supports human editorial decisions, captures notes and tags, builds reel candidates, runs export quality control, and generates client-ready review packages.

The goal is not just to create clips. The goal is to help video teams move from raw footage to organized selects and professional handoff deliverables faster.
```
