# VisionReview AI — LinkedIn Demo Caption

I’ve been building VisionReview AI, a local beta AI-assisted video review and client handoff workflow.

The goal is to help video teams move from raw footage to reviewed selects, draft structure, QC notes, and client-ready deliverables faster.

The current v1.1.2 build includes:

- Segment-level video review
- Human-in-the-loop clip decisions
- Notes and tagging
- Reel candidate planning
- Export quality control
- Client/project metadata
- Client review HTML
- ZIP handoff packages
- Send-ready delivery summaries

What I’m most interested in is the workflow layer around AI video tools.

Most AI video products focus on generating clips. VisionReview AI focuses on helping editors understand footage, organize decisions, and communicate those decisions clearly.

This project combines my background in AI/ML, software development, and video production.
