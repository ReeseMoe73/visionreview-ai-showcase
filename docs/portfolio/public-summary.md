# VisionReview AI — Public Summary

## One-Sentence Description

VisionReview AI is an AI-assisted editorial review and client handoff workflow that helps video teams move from raw footage to reviewed selects, draft structure, QC notes, and client-ready deliverables.

## Short Description

VisionReview AI analyzes uploaded video, organizes it into reviewable segments, supports manual review decisions, captures notes and tags, builds reel candidates, runs export quality checks, and generates client-facing handoff packages.

## What Makes It Different

Most AI video tools focus on generating clips.

VisionReview AI focuses on the workflow around the clips:

```text
review, selection, tagging, quality control, and client delivery
```
