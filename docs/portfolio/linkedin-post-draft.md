# VisionReview AI — LinkedIn Launch/Update Post Draft

I’ve been building VisionReview AI, a local beta AI-assisted video review and client handoff workflow.

The goal is simple:

Help video teams move from raw footage to reviewed selects, draft structure, QC notes, and client-ready deliverables faster.

Instead of positioning it as another AI clip generator, I’ve been focusing on the actual editorial workflow:

- Segment-level video review
- Human-in-the-loop clip decisions
- Notes and tagging
- Reel candidate planning
- Export quality control
- Client/project metadata
- Client review HTML
- ZIP-based handoff packages
- Send-ready delivery summaries

The current local beta is now at v1.1.2, with database-backed client metadata, manual save workflow, last-saved timestamp, and several regression/layout refinements.

This project sits at the intersection of AI/ML, video production, editorial decision-making, and client delivery workflows.

The broader vision:

AI should not just generate clips. It should help editors understand footage, organize decisions, and communicate those decisions clearly.
