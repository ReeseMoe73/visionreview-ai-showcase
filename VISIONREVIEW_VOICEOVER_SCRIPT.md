# VisionReview AI — Voiceover Script

## Opening

VisionReview AI is a local AI-assisted video review and editorial workflow application.

It helps move from raw footage to reviewed selects, reel drafts, QC notes, and export-ready handoff packages.

---

## Problem

Video editors and creators often spend a lot of time scrubbing footage, finding strong moments, organizing clips, writing notes, preparing captions, and packaging deliverables.

VisionReview AI brings those steps into one guided workflow.

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard lets me upload a new video, reopen previous reports, search and filter projects, sort recent videos, and manage local test projects.

---

## Upload and Analysis

After uploading an MP4, the backend processes the footage, extracts metadata, creates segments, analyzes the video, generates transcript information, and returns a structured editorial report.

---

## Report

The report page combines the video player, segment map, transcript excerpts, highlight scores, review controls, and export tools.

---

## Human Review

The AI supports the workflow, but the human editor remains in control.

I can mark segments as Favorite, Keep, Reject, or Unreviewed, then add notes and tags for later editing and export decisions.

---

## Review Mode

Review Mode creates a more focused experience for quickly reviewing segments, applying quick decisions, adding quick tags, and moving through the review queue.

---

## Reel Builder

The Reel Builder turns reviewed clips into structured draft options.

It includes planning, clip order, delivery presets, client review summaries, timeline notes, posting copy, handoff briefs, candidate comparison, and QC.

---

## QC

The QC tab checks whether the draft is ready for export by reviewing runtime, clip count, hook quality, CTA presence, speech, visual warnings, and approval status.

---

## Export

Once the draft is ready, VisionReview AI can export clips, reels, review summaries, CSV files, JSON reports, and a full project package.

The project package includes report data, review segments, transcript, summary, and media inventory.

---

## Closing

VisionReview AI combines applied AI, computer vision, transcription, media processing, full-stack development, and editorial workflow design.

This is currently a Local Beta v1.0. The public GitHub repository is a showcase version, while the full working source code remains private as I evaluate the project’s commercial direction.
