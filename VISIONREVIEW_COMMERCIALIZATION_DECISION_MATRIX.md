## Interpretation

Best immediate value:

```text
Portfolio + SkreenKings internal tool
```

Best future commercial path:

```text
Client review / editor handoff product
```

Most complex path:

```text
Full SaaS
```

---

## Recommended Decision

Do not build SaaS until at least one of these is true:

- 5+ real users request it
- 3+ users say they would pay
- SkreenKings workflows prove time savings
- A clear niche emerges
- The client review/export package becomes clearly valuable

---

## Minimum Validation Questions

Ask potential users:

1. How do you currently review raw footage?
2. How long does it take to find selects?
3. Do you use transcripts in your workflow?
4. How do you organize client notes?
5. What do you send clients for review?
6. Would a tool like this save time?
7. Which feature matters most: analysis, review, captions, QC, or exports?
8. Would you prefer local or cloud?
9. What would you pay monthly or per project?
10. What would make this unusable for you?
